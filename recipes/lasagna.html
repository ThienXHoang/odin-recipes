Lasanga Recipe

Description of Recipe

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: itâ€™s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1 lb Italian sausage (casings removed)
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
    • 1.5 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
    • Pinch of sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • For the Cheese Mixture:
    • 1 (15 oz) carton ricotta cheese
    • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for topping
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • For Assembly:
    • 9-12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
    • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps

    Prepare the sauce:

    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add ground beef, Italian sausage, and diced onion. Cook until the meat is browned and the onion is translucent, breaking the meat into small pieces. Drain any excess fat.
    2. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
    3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water (if using). Season with parsley, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and an optional pinch of sugar.

    Prepare the Cheese Mixture and Noodles:

    1. While the sauce simmers, in a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
    2. If using regular noodles, cook them in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package directions until *al dente*. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking. (Skip this step if using no-boil noodles).

    Bake and Serve

    1. Cover the baking dish tightly with aluminum foil (spray the underside of the foil with non-stick spray to prevent the cheese from sticking).
    2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
    4. Let the lasagna **rest for at least 15 minutes** before slicing and serving. This is crucial for the layers to set and prevents it from being soupy.
